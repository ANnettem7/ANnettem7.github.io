Kittens!

Kittens are very sensitive and fragile animals, the food that they eat and the products that they use are very different than an adult cat.In this page I will list some: food, and hygene products/steps fit for kittens.

Food:

  1. Fancy Feast Kitten Canned Food
  2. Iams ProActive Kitten Dry Food
  3. Hill's Science Diet Wet cat Food

Hygiene:

  1. Any fragrance free kitten shampoo
  2. Do not directly add shampoo into their fur, use a damp towel and spread it out that way
  3. Dry with towel
  4. Do not apply water near ear area
  5. Well & Good Botanicals Soften & Replenish Coconut Cat Wipes
